# 👻 Ghost Protocol — Privacy Series

> *"They can't track what doesn't exist."*

A public, open-source privacy education series by [@brainsinha](https://instagram.com/brainsinha).  
Each episode pairs with an Instagram Reel/Carousel and documents the exact privacy steps covered — so anyone can follow along, verify, and implement.

---

## 📌 What is this?

Ghost Protocol is a privacy-centric content series targeting tech-savvy Indian users.  
This repo is the **technical companion** to the series — every reel maps to a folder here with:

- The **privacy steps** covered in that episode
- **Tool recommendations** with links
- **Verification methods** (how to confirm it's actually working)
- **Threat level** rating per step
